Training

Training Methodology

 

1st Year  - Soft Skill and Maths Speed concept Training

  • Ice Breaking Exercises
  • Word formation
  • Logical Puzzles
  • Confident Building exercise
  • Memory Training  games

2nd Year – Communication and Basic concepts in Aptitude

  • Ice breaking session
  • One minute talk about him/her self
  • Presentation - Needs of communication
  • Inspiration games
  • Self grooming
  • Giving motivation and inspirations to break their stage fear
  • Listening skill practice – communication lab
  • Motivation session, Placement Importance
  • Attitude Building session
  • Importance Grammar session
  • Presentation Skill Session
  • Basic Grammar(Past, Present & Future) Tenses
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Rearrange the Sentence
  • Prepositions

3rd Year – Language Training and Soft Skill training

  • Training on Language like Writing, Speaking ,  Listening&Reading Skill.
  • Career Planning & Goal Setting
  • Importance of placement  & Placement @ Sasurie
  • Self Grooming
  • Resume Preparation
  • Communication Importance
  • Industry Expectation
  • One Minute Talk
  • GD Do’s & Don’ts
  • Interview Do’s & Don’ts
  • Mock GD Practices

4th Year – Language Training , Soft Skill Training and Campus placement Preparatory Training

  • Training on Language like Writing, Speaking ,  Listening& Reading Skill.
  • Industry Expectation
  • Campus Placement Preparatory Training
  • Higher Studies awareness session

Our Expert Presence :

  1. Tech Mahindra FDP programmes & SDP actrivities
